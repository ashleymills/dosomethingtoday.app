Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a new suggestion?

You receive one suggestion per week. Accept it and complete the activity, then come back next week for a new one.

Can I change my suggestion?

Yes, you can skip once to get a different suggestion. If you skip again, you'll need to wait until the following week for a new one.

How do I add a photo?

After marking an activity as complete, you can add a photo by tapping "Add Photo" on the completion screen. You can take a new photo or choose one from your library.

How do I share my activity?

After completing an activity, tap "Share Progress" to share to Instagram Stories, Snapchat, TikTok, or other apps.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove your account and all associated data.

Is my data backed up?

Yes, your activity history and photos are synced to the cloud. If you sign in on a new device, your data will be restored automatically.
